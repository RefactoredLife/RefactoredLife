## Featured Project: Visualize

**Visualize** is a financial intelligence platform for transforming raw portfolio, transaction, and statement data into decision-ready analytics.

### What it does
- Portfolio analytics across accounts with return, drawdown, volatility, Sharpe ratio, and benchmark tracking
- Tax-aware workflows with capital gains analysis and lot selection support
- Monte Carlo forecasting and retirement planning scenarios
- Expense intelligence with category, merchant, and trend-based analysis
- Statement and transaction ingestion tied into interactive dashboards

### Why it stands out
- Connects ingestion, analytics, modeling, and visualization in one product
- Combines finance domain knowledge with production-oriented engineering
- Designed as a real decision-support system, not just a static dashboard

**Tech:** Python, Streamlit, Plotly, Pandas, SciPy, MariaDB, MongoDB, Google APIs, Docker
